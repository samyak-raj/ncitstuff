Football Basics

What is Football?

Football, also known as soccer in some countries, is a team sport played between two teams of 11 players on a rectangular field. The objective is to score goals by getting a ball into the opposing team's net.

History of Football

Football has ancient origins but modern football, as we know it today, originated in England in the late 19th century. The first official rules were written in 1863, and the sport quickly spread across the globe.

Basic Rules

  • A match consists of two 45-minute halves.
  • Players cannot use their hands or arms to touch the ball (except the goalkeeper within their penalty area).
  • The team with the most goals at the end of the match wins.

Famous Footballers

  • Pele: Widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time.
  • Diego Maradona: Famous for his skill, especially the "Hand of God" goal.
  • Lionel Messi: Known for his incredible dribbling and playmaking abilities.
