Basketball Basics

What is Basketball?

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport played between two teams of five players each. The objective is to score points by shooting the ball through the opposing team's hoop.

History of Basketball

Basketball was invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith in Springfield, Massachusetts. It quickly grew in popularity and is now one of the most watched sports worldwide.

Basic Rules

  • A game consists of four quarters, each lasting 12 minutes.
  • Points are scored by making field goals (2 or 3 points) or free throws (1 point).
  • The team with the most points at the end of the game wins.

Famous Basketball Players

  • Michael Jordan: Often regarded as the greatest basketball player of all time.
  • LeBron James: A modern basketball icon known for his versatility and leadership.
  • Kobe Bryant: Famous for his skill, work ethic, and five NBA championships.

Conclusion

Basketball is an exciting and dynamic sport that combines athleticism, strategy, and teamwork, making it beloved by millions around the world.
